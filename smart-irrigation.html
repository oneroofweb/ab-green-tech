Smart Irrigation & Automation

Wi-Fi sprinkler controllers and automated systems that adjust watering schedules based on real-time weather data.

Intelligent Water Management

Traditional irrigation systems often waste water by operating on fixed timers, regardless of the actual weather conditions. At AB Green Tech, our Smart Irrigation Systems solve this problem by integrating advanced IoT technology into your landscaping.

By using Wi-Fi-enabled controllers, soil moisture sensors, and live weather forecasting, our systems ensure your plants get exactly the right amount of water at the right time. This not only promotes healthier, greener landscapes but also significantly reduces water waste and utility bills.

Core Features

Weather Adaptive

Automatically adjusts watering schedules based on local rain and temperature forecasts.

App Control

Monitor and control your entire irrigation system directly from your smartphone or tablet.

Leak Detection

Smart flow meters detect broken pipes or sprinkler heads and send instant alerts.

Zone Customization

Set different watering parameters for grass, shrubs, and trees based on their specific needs.
