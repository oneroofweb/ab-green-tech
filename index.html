About AB GREENTECH

AB Greentech – Leading Water Solution Company in Punjab & India

AB Greentech is a professional and trusted company offering advanced solutions in irrigation systems, swimming pools, and decorative water features across Punjab and all over India. We specialize in designing, supplying, and executing modern water-based projects that enhance landscapes, gardens, residential properties, and commercial spaces.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology, engineering precision, and creative design to build efficient, durable, and visually appealing water systems. From initial planning and technical drawings to civil work, installation, automation, and equipment supply, we deliver complete end-to-end solutions under one roof.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, innovative, and long-lasting water solutions that improve both functionality and aesthetics of modern outdoor environments across India.
