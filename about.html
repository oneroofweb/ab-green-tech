Irrigation, Fountain & Pool Design Consultancy

At AB Greentech, we offer professional design consultancy services for irrigation systems, water fountains, and swimming pools. Our expert team works closely with architects, engineers, and property owners to create efficient, sustainable, and visually appealing water solutions.

Our services include project analysis, feasibility studies, cost estimation, AutoCAD designs, hydraulic calculations, and detailed technical planning. We also assist during construction with site inspections and final execution support.

With a focus on quality and unbiased guidance, we ensure every design is tailored to your project goals, delivering reliable, cost-effective, and long-lasting results.

Irrigation System Design & Consulting Services

At AB Greentech, we provide expert irrigation design and consulting services focused on sustainable and efficient water management. Our team specializes in designing advanced sprinkler and drip irrigation systems tailored to landscapes of all sizes.

With strong expertise in hydraulics, AutoCAD planning, and real-world installation practices, we create systems that are practical, cost-effective, and climate-friendly. We also design smart and automatic irrigation solutions to reduce water wastage and improve performance.

Our goal is to deliver reliable, high-quality designs while guiding clients at every step, ensuring a successful and sustainable irrigation project.

Process Of Irrigation System Design

Analysis of site irrigation requirement with regional climatic condition

Analysis of Plants water requirement

Project feasibility study & Budget feasibility study

Study of Landscape Drawings & other civil drawings

Prepare irrigation AutoCAD drawings and irrigation hydraulics calculations.

Prepare technical specification of irrigation products

Prepare tender performance specifications and bill of quantity.

Coordination with MEP engineers and site supervisors

Fountain Design Consultancy Services

At AB Greentech, we provide expert fountain design consultancy services, combining creativity with advanced engineering. Our team specializes in hydraulic planning, fluid dynamics, and technical design to create efficient and visually stunning water features.

We work closely with architects, designers, and engineers from concept to completion, ensuring smooth execution. Our services include MEP design, AutoCAD drawings, technical specifications, BOQ preparation, and project management.

Using modern technology, lighting, and automation, we design both dynamic and natural water features. Our focus on sustainability ensures energy-efficient, eco-friendly solutions that transform spaces into elegant and functional environments.

Swimming Pool Design Consultancy Services

At AB Greentech, we offer professional swimming pool design consultancy focused on both aesthetics and strong engineering. We ensure that every hidden element—plumbing, filtration, electrical systems, and mechanical components—is designed for long-term durability and smooth performance.

Our expert team provides complete MEP design, structural planning, and detailed CAD drawings. We prioritize safety, efficiency, and low maintenance while creating visually appealing pool designs.

With a focus on quality, functionality, and energy efficiency, we deliver reliable and well-planned pool designs that meet both technical and aesthetic requirements.
